Phase 01 — Accra Node 0.1

Solvinter Edge begins with one practical node in Accra.

The long-term vision is a network of local digital infrastructure: places for work, learning, making, planning and experimentation.

The first economic engine is a small workstation room where two to four people perform global digital work.
