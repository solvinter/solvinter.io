Nodes

Draft · Version 0.1

Solvinter develops through local nodes rather than one central campus. Each node is built around people first, then infrastructure, then architecture. Every node should be capable of teaching the next one.

Core Principle

People

Develop capable people before building larger infrastructure.

Documentation

Every successful node documents its process for future locations.

Architecture

Buildings should encourage calm, dignity, learning and collaboration.

Planned Development

Cow Lane · Accra

Prototype node. Workspace, networking, documentation and AI-assisted digital work.

Mumford

Possible coastal learning and community node.

Winneba

Potential collaboration with local education and maker activities.

Apam

Exploration of local entrepreneurship and distributed work.

Cape Coast

Regional collaboration, education and technology.

Volta Region

Long-term expansion focused on local partnerships and sustainable energy.

Shared Values

  • Human dignity
  • Documentation first
  • Ethical technology
  • Beautiful workspaces
  • Climate-adapted architecture
  • Local ownership
  • Education through practice
