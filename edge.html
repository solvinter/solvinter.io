Solvinter Edge A small economic observatory for human digital work, distributed compute and documentation-first infrastructure. Vision Cow Lane first. Future nodes later. The goal is to test whether small, well-documented workstations can create real income before larger infrastructure is built. Budget Phase 0 is deliberately small. Two workstations, connectivity, basic power, training time and documentation. Work The first work categories are: - AI-assisted digital tasks - documentation - translation and writing - research support - lightweight technical operations Principle No large claims before logs. No scaling before evidence. Documentation first.
