Solvinter Edge

A small economic observatory for human digital work, distributed compute and documentation-first infrastructure.

Vision

Cow Lane first. Future nodes later. The goal is to test whether small, well-documented workstations can create real income before larger infrastructure is built.

Budget

Phase 0 is deliberately small. Two workstations, connectivity, basic power, training time and documentation.

Work

  • AI-assisted digital tasks
  • Documentation
  • Translation and writing
  • Research support
  • Lightweight technical operations

Principle

No large claims before logs. No scaling before evidence. Documentation first.
